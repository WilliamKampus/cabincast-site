Privacy Policy

Effective June 16, 2026

Data collection

CabinCast does not collect, sell, or share personal data. The app is designed to run locally on your iPhone and serve a local viewer page to devices connected to your iPhone Personal Hotspot.

Screen broadcast

CabinCast uses iOS ReplayKit so you can broadcast your screen to the local CabinCast viewer. The screen stream is served locally from your device. CabinCast does not upload your screen content to a CabinCast server.

Local network

CabinCast uses local network access to make the viewer page reachable from another device on your iPhone Personal Hotspot. This traffic is used for the local viewing session.

Purchases

CabinCast may offer an optional Supporter Pack purchase through Apple's StoreKit system. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.

Support

If you contact support, any information you choose to include in your email is used only to respond to your request.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@wirurun.com.
