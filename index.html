Local iPhone to Tesla mirroring Updated June 17, 2026

Mirror your iPhone screen to the Tesla browser over Personal Hotspot.

CabinCast starts an iOS ReplayKit screen broadcast, serves a local viewer page from your iPhone, and lets the Tesla browser watch it through your iPhone Personal Hotspot.

See Setup Get Support

Setup

1

Start casting on iPhone

Tap Start Casting in CabinCast and approve the iOS broadcast or VPN prompt if it appears.

2

Join the hotspot

Connect the Tesla Wi-Fi to the same iPhone Personal Hotspot that is running CabinCast.

3

Open the viewer

In the Tesla browser, open http://240.3.3.3:8080. Use http://, not HTTPS.

Modes

Included

Free Modes

Stable Mode and Quality Mode keep the basic mirroring path simple at 720p/30. Start mirroring while the Tesla is parked for the most reliable free-mode start.

Optional

Supporter Modes

PRO Codec unlocks the newer tuning lane, High Quality at 900p/30, experimental Smooth Mode at 900p/60, true fullscreen, manual aspect ratio, accent colors, and expert controls.

Support

Built around the Tesla browser's limits.

CabinCast presents through browser-safe canvas paths, includes stream reset controls, and shows heat or quality warnings when the phone needs a lower load. Audio stays on the iPhone or Tesla Bluetooth.

  • View-only: CabinCast cannot control iPhone apps from the Tesla screen.
  • DRM-protected video may appear black because iOS blocks protected screen capture.
  • Use the Tesla browser only while parked and according to local laws.
