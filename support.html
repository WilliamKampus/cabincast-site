Support

Most CabinCast issues come down to the hotspot, the exact viewer URL, selected mode, or phone heat during higher-quality streaming.

Viewer URL http://240.3.3.3:8080

Connection checklist

  • Confirm the Tesla is connected to the iPhone Personal Hotspot.
  • Use http://240.3.3.3:8080, not https://.
  • Approve the iOS local network or VPN prompt if it appears.
  • Restart the CabinCast broadcast if the viewer loads but video does not move.

Mode checks

  • Use Stable Mode first when setting up a new car or phone.
  • Switch to Quality Mode only after Stable Mode is moving cleanly.
  • Supporter PRO Codec, High Quality, and Smooth Mode depend on the hotspot link and phone heat.
  • If the phone reports heat warnings, lower quality before testing again.

Fullscreen

  • True fullscreen is a Supporter feature in the Tesla browser.
  • The viewer may show a bright-screen warning before opening fullscreen.
  • If fullscreen opens but the stream stops, return to the viewer and use Reset Stream.

Known limits

  • CabinCast is view-only and cannot control iPhone apps from the Tesla screen.
  • DRM-protected video may appear black because iOS blocks protected screen capture.
  • Use the Tesla browser only while parked and according to local laws.
  • Use Bluetooth or the iPhone for normal audio.

What to include when emailing

Send your iPhone model, iOS version, Tesla software version, selected CabinCast mode, and what happened after opening the viewer URL.
